Simply guess the RGB color indicated in the header!

If you're unfamiliar with RGB colors, the format is like this: RGB(red, green, blue). The higher the number, the more of that color there is in the mix (the highest number is 255).

So RGB(187, 84, 45) has a medium amount of red, a little green and a little blue. So that color should be a darker orange.
